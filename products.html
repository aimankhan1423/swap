Dissolved Air Floatation

Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) is a water treatment process that clarifies waste water by the removal of suspended matter such as oil or solids. The removal is achieved by dissolving air in the water or waste water under pressure and then releasing the air at atmospheric pressure in a flotation tank.
